# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of Developer Website Ideas, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Being respectful and constructive in all communications
- Welcoming portfolios from developers of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting feedback on submitted entries
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Submitting fake, broken, or impersonated portfolio entries
- Using the project to promote spam, scams, or harmful content
- Harassment or discriminatory language in PR descriptions, issues, or comments
- Attempting to inject malicious code, URLs, or scripts into the repository
- Deliberately submitting entries to demean or mock other developers

## Submission Standards

All portfolio entries must:

- Belong to the person submitting the PR
- Be genuine, publicly accessible developer portfolios
- Contain accurate information (name, role, URL)
- Not contain offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content

Entries that violate these standards will be removed without notice, and repeat offenders may be blocked from contributing.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.
